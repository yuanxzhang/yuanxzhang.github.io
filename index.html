Short Bio: Yuan Zhang is an Associate Professor of Computer Science in Fudan University. He received his Ph.D degree from Fudan University in 2014, and Bachelor degree from Nanjing University in 2009. His research interests span all areas in system security and software analysis, especially on Android security, static code analysis, managed runtime. Currently, he co-directs the System Software and Security Laboratory in Fudan University.

To prospective students/postdoctors. If you want to work with me to tackle cutting-edge system security problems, please feel free to drop me an email.
